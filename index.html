Spaghetti Carbonara

Recipe overview

this classic italian pasta dich is rich , creamy and full of flavor it takes only 20 minutes to prepare and is perfect for a quick meal.(servies 2 people)

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g pancetta
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • salt and black pepper

Cooking steps

  1. boil spaghetti in salted water until aldente.
  2. cook pancetta until crispy.
  3. mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.
  4. combine everything with drained pasta while hot.
  5. season with salt and pepper

tips

make sure the pasta is hot when you mix in the egg and cheese mixture for a creamy texture
